About the Portal

A professional agriculture administration experience

AgriSmart AI is designed as a centralized farmer welfare system inspired by Indian e-governance portals. It helps reduce paperwork, improve verification quality, and accelerate service delivery for subsidy, insurance, loan, and complaint workflows.

Scheme Highlights
  • PM-KISAN income support recommendation
  • PMFBY-aligned crop insurance suggestions
  • Kisan Credit Card style loan workflow
  • Tamil Nadu e-Sevai inspired citizen service interface
  • Complaint tracking with admin response management
  • Farmer dashboard with QR identity and weather insight
Bonus Features

Operational Enhancements

  • Loan receipt PDF generation for farmer download
  • Farmer QR identity code for quick dashboard verification
  • Weather API integration with district-level live snapshot
  • AI-based crop recommendation summary for approved farmers
  • SMS notification logging stub for future gateway integration
  • EMI calculator and repayment tracker in the loan module
Future Scope

Planned Smart Agriculture Expansion

  • Advanced ML scoring for disaster risk and claim probability
